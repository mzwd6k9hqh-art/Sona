About Sona Business Helper

This lightweight app provides practical, ready-to-use tools for creators and business owners: a quick analyzer, a content generator, problem-solving guidance, and audience insight tips — all client-side for privacy and speed.

  • No sign-up required
  • Works offline once loaded (client-side)
  • Embed-ready for Whop (iframe friendly)
